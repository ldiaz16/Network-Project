For more information, visit www.avbench.com or email us at info@avbench.com.AvBenchNetwork  Aggressiveness : reflects  the current  competitive  
pressure  experienced  by a specific  aircraft  or subfleet  in the 
airline’s  network  over  the past  24 months . 
This assessment  considers  the percentage  of routes  and their  
corresponding  weight  by seat capacity  in the network  where  
the aircraft  faces  competition . It is categorized  as either  a 
monopoly,  duopoly,  or having  multiple  players,  relative  to 
industry  benchmarks .
A score  of 5 indicates  an average  level  of competitive  pressure,  
while  a higher  score  represents  a more  favorable  environment .
